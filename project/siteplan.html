Site Plan: Rayane's Cinnamon Rolls

Why this name? It highlights the owner’s name (Rayane) for a personal, handcrafted brand identity and clearly communicates the product: cinnamon rolls.

Optional Domain Ideas

  • rayanescinnamonrolls.com.mx
  • rayanesrolls.mx
  • rolesderayane.mx

Site Purpose

The site promotes and sells homemade artisanal cinnamon rolls, providing product information, a photo gallery, and a simple contact/order form. It also demonstrates responsive design, accessibility, and JavaScript interactivity per course objectives.

Scenarios

  • “What flavors and toppings are available and how much do they cost?”
  • “Can you deliver to my neighborhood in Alpuyeca or nearby?”
  • “Do you offer bulk pricing for a party of 20–40 people?”
  • “How can I place an order for tomorrow morning?”
  • “Are there any promotions this week?”
  • “What ingredients do you use? Any options without nuts?”

Color Schema

At least two colors are defined; the plan uses a warm, bakery-inspired palette. Usage is indicated below and applied in this document.

--brand (Cinnamon Brown) — headings, primary buttons, key accents.
--bg (Cream) — page background, section contrast.
--accent (Berry) — call-to-action highlights, links on hover.
--ink (Near-Black) — body text for optimal readability.

Typography

  • Poppins — Headings (H1–H3), logo wordmark.
  • Inter — Body text, forms, navigation.

Both fonts are web-safe via Google Fonts and provide excellent legibility. Fall back to system fonts when unavailable.
