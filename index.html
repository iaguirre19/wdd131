Cuernavaca Morelos, Mexico

Cuernavaca is known as the "City of Eternal Spring" due to its pleasant climate year-round. It's a popular destination for those seeking a warm getaway. The city is rich in history and culture, with attractions like the Palacio de Cortés, a historic palace built by Hernán Cortés, and the beautiful Jardín Borda, a historic garden that offers a peaceful retreat.
