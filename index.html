About Me

Cuernavaca Morelos

My name is Iran Ricardo Aguirre Castañeda. I was born and raised in Mexico City. At the age of 14, I moved with my parents to Cuernavaca, Morelos, where I lived until I was 18. After that, I served a full-time mission in the Mexico Guadalajara East Mission. Upon returning from my mission service, I worked for a telecommunications company for two years and then moved to California to work in IT. I met my wife through a dating app called MUTUAL, and I traveled to Brazil to meet her. We have been married for 4 years now and have a beautiful son.

Cuernavaca, Morelos, is a vibrant city located in central Mexico. Known for its pleasant climate, Cuernavaca is often referred to as the "City of Eternal Spring." It boasts beautiful colonial architecture, lush gardens, and a rich cultural heritage. Many visitors are drawn to its historic landmarks, such as the Palacio de Cortés and the Cuernavaca Cathedral. Additionally, Cuernavaca is famous for its traditional cuisine, including dishes like mole poblano and cochinita pibil.
